About Trunk


Trunk has developed and commercialised a web and mobile platform that underpins a range of business verticals for in the Real Estate, Health and Productivity industries amongst many others. By effectively utilising Microservice architecture, custom elements tailored to a specific industry can be added cost-effectively by Trunk’s Sydney and Melbourne based development teams. We provide technology for some of Australia’s largest Healthcare, Telecommunications and Real Estate companies and are expanding quickly.
